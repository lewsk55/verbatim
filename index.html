How to play

You're shown who said today's quotation. A word or two is already in place. Tap words from the bank to drop them into the blanks, in the order you think the quote was spoken.

Tap Set the line to check. Words in the right spot lock in; the rest come back to the bank. There's no limit on tries — but the fewer moves it takes, the higher you'll place.

Solve it, and the line sets into print to share.
